PC Cabinet Components

Motherboard

The motherboard is the main circuit board that houses the CPU, RAM, and other essential components. It connects all the parts of a computer and allows them to communicate with each other.

CPU (Central Processing Unit)

The CPU is the brain of the computer where most calculations take place. It executes instructions from programs and processes data.

RAM (Random Access Memory)

RAM is the memory used by the CPU to store and access data quickly. It is volatile memory, meaning it loses its content when the power is turned off.

GPU (Graphics Processing Unit)

The GPU is responsible for rendering images and video. It is crucial for gaming and graphic-intensive applications.

PSU (Power Supply Unit)

The PSU converts electrical power from an outlet into a usable form for the other components of the computer.

Storage (HDD/SSD)

Storage devices like HDDs and SSDs are used to store data permanently. SSDs are faster and more reliable than traditional HDDs.
